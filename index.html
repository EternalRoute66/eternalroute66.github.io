The Big Idea

Two Roads. One Journey.

Chuck Williams links a full Route 66 journey from Chicago to Santa Monica with a devotional journey through the 66 books of the Bible. The result is not a travel guide. It is a story of roadbeds, cafés, motels, ghost towns, family, faith, and the pace of a life worth noticing.

“

Two roads have had a profound influence on my life… the road that leads through the 66 books that make up the Bible… and the old highway, known as U.S. Route 66.

— Chuck Williams

Meet Chuck

About the Author

Chuck and Ann Williams are avid travelers of Route 66. Chuck retired as Senior Pastor of Live Oak Community Church in Lubbock, Texas, and now lives in the Dallas area near family. He is available for speaking and teaching engagements — and might even be persuaded to help host a trip across Route 66.

The heart of this site is wide open: Route 66 fans, people of faith, history lovers, seekers, travelers, families, photographers, and anyone who still believes the old road has something to say.
